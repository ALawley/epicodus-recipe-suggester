Teriyaki Zucchini and Onion
Taste of Home
    Ingredients
  • 2 medium zucchini, cut into strips
  • 1 small onion, sliced and separated into rings
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted
